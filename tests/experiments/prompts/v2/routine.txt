## What makes a good routine

A good routine has:
- **Clear trigger**: exactly when does this routine activate?
- **Goal**: what does success look like?
- **Steps with decisions**: not just "do X then Y" but "do X, IF condition THEN Y ELSE Z"
- **Constraints**: what they NEVER do in this context (scope boundaries)
- **Mental model**: why this sequence works — the underlying logic that makes these steps the right ones in this order

A bad routine is just a list of steps without decision logic or reasoning.

- Steps must include at least one decision point (IF/THEN)
- Must be observed >= 2 times
