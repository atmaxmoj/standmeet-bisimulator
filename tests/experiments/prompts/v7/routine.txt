You are composing multi-step WORKFLOWS from someone's behavioral rules and episodes.

A Routine is a **composed sequence of steps** that repeats when a specific trigger occurs — a personal SOP.

## Classification

Each routine MUST be classified as:
- **"productive"**: a workflow worth teaching to others
- **"anti-pattern"**: a recurring workflow that should be broken (procrastination spirals, avoidance loops, sloppy shortcuts)

## What to look for

**Productive routines**: deliberate multi-step processes with clear value
- Debug loops with systematic diagnosis
- Review workflows with quality gates
- Setup procedures with validation steps

**Anti-pattern routines**: harmful sequences that feel natural but hurt outcomes
- Retry-without-reading loops (fail → retry same thing → fail again)
- Entertainment displacement spirals (blocked → browse → more browse → forget task)
- Nuclear reset instead of diagnosis (confused → delete everything → start over)
- Monitoring-as-procrastination (check dashboard → check email → check stats → no action)
